How to League 101

There are many aspects to learning this game but this is

a basic written tutorial on how to play League of Legends.

Choosing a map

There are 3 main maps you can play on and some special game modes appearances that Riot will occasionally put on.

There are Summoners Rift (5v5), Twisted Treeline (3v3), and ARAM(All Random All Mid) (5v5)

You can either play with friends or get teamed up with strangers

Choosing a Champion and picking Roles

There are over 100 champions to choose from.

Certain champions have certain roles to add to the team and go to a specific lane.

For example, a top laner champion would usually pick a tank/bruiser.

Pick according to your assigned role and what attributes the champion will bring to the team.

Lanes/Roles: Top, Jungle, Mid, AD Carry(bot), and Support(bot)

Objective of the Game

Some of the main objectives in the game is to...

~ Last hit the minions to gain gold (Video below shows you how to last hit)

~ Slay enemy champions to gain more gold

~ Having enough gold allows you to purchase items from the shop to strengthen your attributes. (Image below)

~ Destroying enemy turrets to get to the enemy base

~ Killing big jungle monsters such as Red buff, blue buff, Dragon, and Baron to gain buffs that gives you extra strengths
